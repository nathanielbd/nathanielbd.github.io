Photo from AUVSI SUAS

I'm the software director of my university's Copter Club. The student group enters competitions for the autonomous flight, remote sensing, and navigation of multi-rotor copters. I contribute to the computer vision and automation code used for the 2019 AUVSI SUAS competition.

The Sick Shrimp Project

a screencap of the Sick Shrimp Project webapp

I participated in MinneHack 2019, the largest hackathon in the Twin Cities. In less than 24 hours, my group created The Sick Shrimp Project, a webapp that assembles information on various factors for White Spot Virus and Early Mortality Syndrome in aquacultural shrimp and employs a neural network to classify images of shrimp as either 'healthy' or 'infected' by the two ailments.

CSBIO Lab

Genetic Interaction Network of the Human Genome

I'm a volunteer researcher at the CSBIO Lab in the University of Minnesota. The lab focuses on computational biology and functional genomics. I am writing code which finds patterns of genetic interactions in CRISPR-edited HAP1 cells using R and visualizes the data using Cytoscape.
