About

Info on me and this site

This site serves as a sort of portfolio. It has links to the GitHub repos of my personal programming projects as well as a few words about them. It also has a blog where I'll sometimes write about different things I find interesting. I'm always learning outside of the classroom using any MOOC, tutorial, textbook, or walkthrough I can get my hands on over the internet. Recruiters can find a copy of my résumé up top.

I'm currently a 2nd-year undergraduate student at the University of Minnesota where I'm studying computer science, math, and computational biology. I have software engineering internship experience, and I've volunteered my time in a computational biology lab. I am interested in every quantitative discipline, especially those that can be applied to human and public health. However, the field that most engrosses me at the moment is computational structural biology, especially the unsolved problem of protein structure prediction.

Outside of the spheres of school and career, I love board games, books, TV, and video games. Avalon. Zork. Douglas Adams. Ender's Shadow. NFL. Avatar: The Last Airbender. That's my jam.
