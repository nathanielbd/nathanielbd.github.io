The Sick Shrimp Project

a screencap of the Sick Shrimp Project webapp

I participated in MinneHack 2019, the largest hackathon in the Twin Cities. In less than 24 hours, my group created The Sick Shrimp Project, a webapp that assembles information on various factors for White Spot Virus and Early Mortality Syndrome in aquacultural shrimp and employs a neural network to classify images of shrimp as either 'healthy' or 'infected' by the two ailments.
